What is this thing?

TaxonManager is a tool for classifying fossil species.

I didn’t receive an email after signing up.

Please, check your junk email. Otherwise, contact the administrator.

Why is my access still limited after logging in?

Contributor privileges are required to modify data on this site. Contributor privileges are granted to users as invite only. If one however, feel they possess the required skills and knowledge, to contribute to this site. Then, please contact the administrator, who will make an assessment.
Contributor privileges are in other words not automatically assigned upon request.

TaxonManager participated in University of Helsinki’s Software Development Project course in the spring of 2022. A team consisting of computer science students aided in the development of TaxonManager. The team included the following students: Tuomas Aaltonen, Seppo Kajantie, Juhana Karhunen, Ari Kauppi, Anne Kuivanen and Cecilia Salpaoja.
